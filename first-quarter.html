Characters in Noli Me Tangere
Juan Crisóstomo Ibarra y Magsalin
  • He studied in Europe for 7 years

  • The son of Don Rafael
María Clara
  • She grew up with Ibarra and planned to marry him
  • She is a woman of high social standing
  • She is the biological daughter of Padre Damaso
Elías
  • He is a fugitive living in San Diego
  • His life was saved by Ibarra
  • He works for Ibarra
Padre Dámaso

Padre Salví

  • He is more dangerous and gifted

Kapitan Tiago
  • A wealthy, native-born filipino
  • María Clara's "father"
